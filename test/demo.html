A weekend in numbers

We hiked a 5 mile loop that climbed 1,200 ft, carrying packs that weighed about 22 lb each plus 3 stone of shared gear. The trailhead sign said the summit lake covers 40 acres.

It hit 92°F by noon, so we drank nearly 2 gallons of water between us and topped up with another 16 fl oz each at the spring. On the drive home we cruised at 65 mph on the interstate.

For dinner: a recipe calling for 2 cups of flour, 8 oz butter, baked at 350°F. The new oven is rated to 500 fahrenheit.

These should be ignored: a 5G phone, a 4K monitor, a $20K car.
